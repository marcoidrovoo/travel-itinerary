Travel Itinerary: Medellin & Puerto Vallarta

March 1, 2025 - March 7, 2025

Overview

Duration: 7 Days

Departure: Newark, NJ

Day 1: Fly to Medellin

  • Morning: Flight from Newark (EWR) to Medellin (MDE).
  • Afternoon: Arrival and transfer to El Poblado accommodation.

Day 2: Explore Medellin

  • Morning: Comuna 13 Graffiti Tour (~$20).
  • Afternoon: Arví Park Cable Car (~$10).

Day 3: Fly to Puerto Vallarta

  • Morning: Flight from Medellin (MDE) to Puerto Vallarta (PVR).
  • Evening: Check into Zona Romántica accommodation.

Day 4: Relax in Puerto Vallarta

  • Morning: Beach day at Playa Los Muertos (Free).
  • Afternoon: Snorkeling or boat tour (~$30).

Day 5: Adventure and Tasting

  • Morning: ATV Tour through Puerto Vallarta’s jungle (~$40).
  • Afternoon: Tequila tasting tour at a local distillery (~$30).

Day 6: Fly Back to Newark

  • Morning: Flight from Puerto Vallarta (PVR) to Newark (EWR).

Cost Breakdown

  • Flight (EWR to MDE): $300
  • Accommodation in Medellin: $90
  • Flight (MDE to PVR): $375
  • Accommodation in Puerto Vallarta: $120
  • Flight (PVR to EWR): $200
  • Activities: $160
